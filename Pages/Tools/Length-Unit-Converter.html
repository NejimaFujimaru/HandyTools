Common Length Conversion Factors

Understanding common conversions can be helpful: 1 meter = 3.28084 feet, 1 kilometer = 0.621371 miles, 1 inch = 2.54 centimeters, 1 foot = 12 inches, and 1 yard = 3 feet.
